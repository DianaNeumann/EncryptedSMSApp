Privacy Policy

SpiderSMS utilizes state-of-the-art security and end-to-end encryption to provide private messaging and Internet calling services to users worldwide (“Services”). Your calls and messages are always encrypted, so they can never be shared or viewed by anyone but yourself and the intended recipients.

Information you provide

Account Information. You register a phone number when you create a SpiderSMS account. Phone numbers are used to provide our Services to you and other SpiderSMS users. You may optionally add other information to your accounts, such as a profile name. This information is end-to-end encrypted.

Messages. SpiderSMS cannot decrypt or otherwise access the content of your messages or calls. SpiderSMS queues end-to-end encrypted messages on its servers for delivery to devices that are temporarily offline (e.g. a phone whose battery has died). Your message history is stored on your own devices. 

Contacts. SpiderSMS can optionally discover which contacts in your address book are SpiderSMS users, using a service designed to protect the privacy of your contacts. Information from the contacts on your device may be cryptographically hashed and transmitted to the server in order to determine which of your contacts are registered.

User Support. If you contact SpiderSMS User Support, any personal data you may share with us is kept only for the purposes of researching the issue and contacting you about your case.

Managing your information. You can manage your personal information in SpiderSMS’s application Settings. For example, you can update your profile information or choose to enable additional privacy features like a Registration Lock PIN.
