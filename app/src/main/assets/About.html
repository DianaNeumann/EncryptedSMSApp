About SpiderSMS

SpiderSMS is an End-to-End Encrypted and Tunneling SMS app for the extra security-conscious individual. The purpose of this app is to enable any user to communicate with others securely and covertly by not only encrypting their SMS communications but also tunneling their SMS traffic to a user-defined 3rd party ensuring all SMS communications sent through this app are neither readable and or traceable by either an intercepting party or the telco service provider.
